# Gemini Notebook — Story / Lore / Puzzle Manager

## Role

Own **narrative continuity**, **lore truth**, and **puzzle intent** for *A Door Inside the Dark*.

## What this tool should do

- Maintain stable IDs, room-to-lore links, puzzle dependencies, and Hearth/Shadow/Ego framing in notebook form.
- Produce or refine specs that map cleanly to `Docs/ROOM_SPECS`, `Docs/PUZZLE_SPECS`, and `Docs/LORE_ITEMS` (and templates under `templates/`).
- Flag contradictions, missing beats, and sensitivity concerns before implementation.

## What to avoid

- Unity project edits, C#, or URP configuration.
- Inventing gameplay systems or code architecture; describe *what* should be true in the fiction and design, not *how* singletons or services work.
- Overwriting established canon without calling it out as a deliberate retcon.
